Dele-gator

Your Virtual Delegation Assistant!

Are you having difficulty delegating tasks to the right employees? Let Dele-gator do it for you!

Simply have your employees fill out a survey about their personality and skills. When you have a project to divy out, fill out a survey indicating what kind of a person you want to lead it and Dele-gator will find you the right person for the job!

Don't want to risk biased answers from your employees filling out information about themselves? Or maybe you just don't want them to know what you're doing. Don't worry, Dele-gator lets you fill out surveys for them!

Easily keep track of the talents you have on your team! Simply fill out an Employee Survey for each team member and Dele-gator will store the results. When you have a project to delegate, fill in a Project Survey to find out who the right employee for the job is!
